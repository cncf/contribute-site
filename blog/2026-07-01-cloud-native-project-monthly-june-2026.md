## Mentorship Program Updates

LFX Mentoring has grown into one of the largest open source mentorship programs in the world, connecting new contributors with experienced maintainers across hundreds of projects every year. As the program scales, we're making two updates that keep it fair, sustainable and welcoming for everyone. [Learn more](https://contribute.cncf.io/blog/2026/06/26/strengthening-lfx-mentoring).

## Oracle Cloud Usage Optimization

Nearly 50 projects have adopted Oracle Cloud Infrastructure for ARM compute, CI/CD pipelines, and compatibility testing over the past 3 years. Please review your current usage and shut down any idle or unused resources so we can continue to be conscientious stewards of these donated resources.

## Please Complete the 2H 2026 Maintainer Survey

We're entering the second half of the year, and that means another [maintainer survey](https://maintainers-survey.cncf.io). This is your chance to provide feedback to make sure the CNCF remains the best place for maintainers to host a cloud native project.
