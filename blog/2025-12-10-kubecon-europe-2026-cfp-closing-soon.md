CFPs and the Project Opportunities Interest Form for KubeCon + CloudNativeCon
Europe 2026 are closing soon!
[CFP](https://sessionize.com/project-benefits-kubecon-eu-2026/) for Maintainer
Track, Contribfest, and Project Lightning Talks closes on Sunday, 14 December,
and the
[Kiosk + PR Interest Form](https://linuxfoundation.research.net/r/ProjectOpps2026)
closes on Wednesday, 17 December, both at 23:59 local time.

Planning to speak at the
[Maintainer Summit](https://events.linuxfoundation.org/kubecon-cloudnativecon-europe/features-add-ons/maintainer-summit/)?
Be sure to [submit your CFP](https://sessionize.com/maintainer-summit-eu-2026)
by Sunday, 14 December at 23:59 local time.

Please visit the
[Project Opportunities page](https://events.linuxfoundation.org/kubecon-cloudnativecon-europe/features-add-ons/project-opportunities/)
to learn more about the benefits, deadlines, how to sign up, and more.

Kindly note that due to high demand, space is limited and there is a high
possibility that not all projects will be able to participate as we are not able
to accept all requests.
